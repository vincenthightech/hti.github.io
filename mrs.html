Modular Recording System (MRS)

Stackable channels, field-serviceable modules, and portable packaging for seafloor or buoy deployments.

  • Modular channels
  • 24-bit acquisition
  • Battery or shore power

Overview

Concise summary of use cases and why MRS is chosen. Note servicing approach, endurance, and scaling.

Applications

  • Seafloor noise and event monitoring
  • Multi-channel seismic/acoustic surveys
  • Long-duration autonomous studies

What’s Included

  • Recorder chassis with N channels
  • Battery pack(s) or power interface
  • Comms/Sync module and cabling

Options & Accessories

  • Channel count: 8 / 16 / 32 / 64+
  • Storage: 1–16 TB
  • GNSS or PTP time sync
  • Pressure housing depth rating

BandwidthDC to 50 kHz (per channel, front-end dependent)
Sample Rate1 kS/s – 192 kS/s, per channel

StorageUp to 16 TB removable
TimingGNSS, PTP, or oven-controlled oscillator
